Exclusive Natural Mineral Water

The taste of thousands of years

One of the most remarkable water features of the Andes is Colico Lake in Chile, which is fed by a natural stream from the foothills of the mountains. The lake has clear and pure water that reflects the beauty of the surrounding landscape. The stream originates near the Llaima Volcano, one of the most active volcanoes in Chile. The water is so pristine that it is bottled and sold as Andes Mountain Water, a fine bottled water that claims to have a balanced mineral composition and a refreshing taste.
